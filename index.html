The Professional

The Perspective of an Owner,
The Passion of a PGA Professional

I view the golf industry through a unique dual lens: the hospitality of a PGA Professional and the discerning eye of a business owner. Currently serving at Desert Forest Golf Club, I specialize in delivering concierge level member and guest service while maintaining the rigorous operational standards required at a premier facility.

My career path distinguishes me from the traditional golf professional. After early successes at prestigious venues like Tavistock Country Club and The ACE Club, I spent a decade in the executive seat founding and operating my own company, LoLo Juice. That experience provided an education in self reliance and dedication, building a successful culture, driving revenue growth, and managing strict inventory controls.
