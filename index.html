The Professional

The Perspective of an Owner,
The Passion of a PGA Professional

I view the golf industry through a unique dual lens: the hospitality of a PGA Professional and the discerning eye of a business owner. Currently serving at Desert Forest Golf Club, I specialize in delivering concierge level member and guest service while maintaining the rigorous operational standards required at a premier facility.

My career path distinguishes me from the traditional golf professional. After early successes at prestigious venues like Tavistock Country Club and The ACE Club, I spent a decade in the executive seat founding and operating my own company, LoLo Juice.

Today, I combine that executive acumen with modern operational expertise, bringing an owner’s mentality to the operation and a servant’s heart to the member experience.

Leading with a Servant's Heart

Leadership Philosophy

I don’t just manage operations; I serve as a steward of the club’s culture. Leadership requires a dual focus: internally, I empower the staff to execute the details that define the club’s excellence; externally, I engage the membership through visibility, personal connection, and a deep respect for the club’s traditions. The son of a coach, my approach to leadership has deep roots and has been honed by years in high-end golf operations and a decade as a business operator.

Servant Leadership

Effective authority isn't about giving orders; it's about removing obstacles. Clear communication is the foundation of building a high-performing team environment, but that environment is sustained by action. I lead from the front, modeling the work ethic, presentation, and attention to detail I expect from my staff. My goal is to serve the team with the clarity and support they need, ensuring they have the confidence to anticipate needs and deliver superior service to members and guests.

Mentorship & Development

I have found that the most rewarding aspect of leadership is lifting others up—championing their goals because their success is my success. My mentorship extends across every pillar of the operation, providing comprehensive training in Tournament Operations, Teaching and Coaching, Merchandising, and Business Management. By investing in their diverse interests, I not only build a high-performing team today but help shape the industry leaders of tomorrow.

Instruction

Developing the Player, Not Just the Swing

Effective golf instruction must be uniquely tailored to the individual. Beginning with the fundamentals of grip, posture and alignment, my instruction philosophy is not a "one-size-fits-all" model. I prioritize understanding a player’s fitness levels and physical restrictions before making any swing corrections that fit their specific body. When necessary, I utilize launch monitor and video analysis, to provide students with clear, data-backed insights that bridge the gap between "feel" and "real".

  • → Utilizing Trackman data and video analysis to provide students with actionable, technical clarity.
  • → Training a repeatable, effective motion tailored to the student's personal biomechanics and physical capabilities.

Programming & Revenue Impact: Beyond individual lessons, I specialize in designing high-engagement programs like "Gals & Guests" and specialized Short Game Schools. These initiatives are designed to do more than lower scores; they are strategic tools that drive participation across club departments, from guest fee revenue to increased Food & Beverage activity.

Junior Golf Leadership: I have played an integral role for large-scale junior programs, including at Tavistock Country Club. My approach focuses on creating a fun and engaging environment that fosters a lifelong love for the game while building the next generation of active club members.

The Mental Game & Performance: I believe that while golf strategy is refined through analytics, athletic performance is sustained by a clear mind. My coaching bridges the gap between the range and the first tee by emphasizing a transition from conscious mechanics to instinctive execution. By refining a student's pre-shot routines and state of mind, I empower them to step over the ball with a singular focus: picking a target and delivering the shot.

  • → Designing cross-functional player development programs that drive revenue in Golf and F&B.
  • → Managing comprehensive junior pipelines that engage families and secure the club's future membership.
  • → Coaching the transition from mechanical thought to target-oriented performance for improved on-course scoring.
